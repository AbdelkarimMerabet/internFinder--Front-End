Frequently Asked Questions

On this page are the most frequently asked questions

Can I cancel the advertisement after it is published?

The advertisement can be canceled after it has been published, provided that all the organizers of the training course are notified.

Why are students evaluated after completing the training internship?

Improving the student's CV and increasing the chances of admission to participate in future training internship

On what basis is the student (trainee) evaluated?

The student is assessed on the basis of learning discipline and capacity development

Is it possible to withdraw from the internship after its start?

Of course. You can withdraw from the internship after its start, provided that the owner of the company agrees

Why do I have to evaluate companies after the end of the internship?

To improve the content of future training internship

On what basis is the internship evaluated?

Training courses are evaluated on the basis of internship content and privileges (catering, transportation).
